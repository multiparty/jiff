jiff-instance.protocols

generate_and_share_random(thresholdopt, receivers_listopt, senders_listopt, Zpopt, share_idopt) → {SecretShare}

Creates shares of an unknown random number. Every party comes up with its own random number and shares it. Then every party combines all the received shares to construct one share of the random unknown number.

 receivers_list.length the min number of parties needed to reconstruct the secret, defaults to all the receivers.

 all_parties array of party ids to share with, by default, this includes all parties.
