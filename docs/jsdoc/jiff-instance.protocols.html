Namespace: protocols

jiff-instance.protocols

A collection of useful protocols to be used during computation or preprocessing: extensions are encouraged to add useful common protocols here, under a sub namespace corresponding to the extension name.

generate_and_share_random(thresholdopt, receivers_listopt, senders_listopt, Zpopt) → {secret-share}

Creates shares of an unknown random number. Every party comes up with its own random number and shares it. Then every party combines all the received shares to construct one share of the random unknown number.
